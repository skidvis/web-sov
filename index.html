Step out of the Shadows of Villainy.


A cataclysmic event has created a new breed of villians who are causing havoc. Thankfully you, and a select few, also have cool new elemental powers to be used for the good of humanity.

Crypto-Based?

A limited print of the game's core villains will be available on OpenSea. The first seventy NFTs will grant access to a special discord channel where key decisions will be discussed.
In essence, owners of the first 70 will contribute to the game's development and lore.

Every NFT in the set will grant the player an in-game bonus based on the card's rarity and element.

About the game

  • Fight villains and criminals in this 3rd-person Superhero game.
  • Complete missions to earn Kudos which can be exchanged for ShatterCalls.
  • Each ShatterCall has the potential to unlock an upgrade or a new playable character NFT.
  • Don't want the unlocked character?
    Trade your NFT with other players!

Who are you?

This is a SharkJets production, led by Skid Vis.
SharkJets teaches devs how to make games using Unity on the SharkJets YouTube channel.

SharkJets previously created the Web3 title, DragonMound, which rewards players who finish it with a DragonMound Pioneer NFT and is an active participant in the EverDragons 2 community, rewarding GoldMine players with a fancy GoldMiner NFT.

Owning these NFTs may result in in-game perks/cosmetics. *wink wink*
